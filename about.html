    About Us

    Bardhaman Eye Care Nursing Home was established by leading Ophthalmologist Dr. Sourav Ghosh. We aim to set a new benchmark for providing quality, consistent, and comprehensive eye care services. We are committed to making our services affordable and accessible to all.

    At Bardhaman Eye Care, we believe in providing the best possible care to our patients. Our surgeons are equipped with the latest and most advanced diagnostic and surgical technology, allowing us to accurately diagnose and treat a wide range of eye problems safely and effectively.

    Strategically located at Parbirhata on the Ground and Second floor, RG Bhawan, Bardhaman Eye Care Nursing Home offers easy access to patients from all parts of East Bardhaman and neighboring districts including West Bardhaman, Hooghly, Howrah, Birbhum, Bankura, Purulia, Murshidabad. Jharkhand.

    Our Vision:

    At Bardhaman Eye Care Nursing Home, our vision is to revolutionize eye care by setting a new standard of excellence in ophthalmology. Led by the expertise of Dr. Sourav Ghosh, we aspire to be the leading beacon of cure for pre-existing eye problems. Our mission is to offer unparalleled eye care services that are not only innovative and effective but also affordable and convenient for everyone.

    Our mission:

    At Bardhaman Eye Care Nursing Home, we aim to provide exceptional and comprehensive eye care with a commitment to quality, precision, and compassion. We are dedicated to using the latest technology and advanced surgical techniques to provide accurate diagnosis and effective treatment for a wide range of eye conditions.
