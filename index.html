    Director's message

    As the Director of Bardhaman Eye Care Nursing Home, I am extremely proud to launch our state-of-the-art facility dedicated to providing unparalleled eye care services. Our mission is to set a new benchmark in ophthalmology by ensuring quality, consistency, and comprehensive care that is affordable and accessible to everyone.

    Our highly skilled surgeons are equipped with the latest diagnostic and surgical technology, enabling us to diagnose and treat eye problems with precision, safety, and efficacy. We are committed to being at the forefront of medical advances to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.

    Strategically located on the Ground and Second floor, RG Bhawan in Parbirhata, Bardhaman Eye Care Nursing Home is easily accessible to patients from East Bardhaman and neighboring districts including West Bardhaman, Hooghly, Howrah, Birbhum, Bankura, Purulia, Murshidabad and Jharkhand. Our location ensures that high-quality eye care is within reach of a wider community, improving the region's overall healthcare infrastructure.

    We pride ourselves on offering various advanced eye care treatments at the most affordable prices. Our mission is to meet and exceed the expectations of our valued patients, providing them with the best quality service in a welcoming and professional environment.
