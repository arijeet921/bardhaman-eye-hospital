    Cataract Services

    Cataract Services

    Cataract is one of the leading cause of blindness in INDIA. It can be treated by a simple cataract extraction phacoemulsification surgery, which is performed using advanced technology in a short time. Bardhaman Eye Care is fully equipped with all the most modern technology for performing phacosurgery.
    1. World's No.1 "CENTURION VISION SYSTEM' for performing cataract surgery through 2.2 mm incision with implantation of foldable IOL of premium quality.
    2.IOL power calculation performed by high precision non contact optical biometer ARGOS which is a swept source OCT based biometry that provides accurate results.
    3.'Verion' (Image Guided Cataract Surgery) to minimise refractive error customised for each patient. This innovative technology brings a completely different light to surgery.
    4.World class ophthalmic microscope with best illumination and depth of field.
    5.Wide variety of Lens which corrects all type of refractive error (reducing glass dependancy) as per patient needs and lifestyle.

    Glaucoma

    Glaucoma

    Bardhaman Eye Care offers comprehensive Glaucoma diagnosis and treatment.
    Our diagnostics are equipped with Perimetry, OCT (3D) for RNFL thickness analysis, and YAG laser for PI.
    We perform Glaucoma surgeries (Trabeculectomy with MMC) for arrest of Glaucoma.

    Retina

    Retina

    The VitreoRetina Department of Bardhaman Eye Care deals with management of Diabetic Retinopathy, Retinal Vein occlusion, ARMD, Retinal Detachment, Infection, Trauma, and other Retina disorders.
    Our Diagnostics are equipped with advanced trauma machines, like OCT (3D), Green laser, USG-B scan.
    Our OT is equipped with world's most advanced Constellation Vision System (Alcon, USA).

    Oculoplasty

    Oculoplasty

    Bardhaman Eye Care Oculoplasty Service deals with various diseases of eyelids and lacrimal system.
    Our Surgeon routinely performs Oculoplastic procedures like DCT, DCR, Ectropion, Entropion, lid Surgery (ptosis) Enucleation/Evisceration, Botox Injection to treat all types of disorders.

    Paediatric Ophthalmology
    Paediatric Ophthalmology

    Bardhaman Eye Care Paediatric Ophthalmology service involved diagnosis and management of paediatric eye disorders like Refractive Error, Childhood Cataract, Glaucoma, Amblyopia, Squint, Congenital Anomalies, Retinal disorders.
    The Centre has a variety of equipments necessary to perform an extensive paediatric ophthalmic examination. We approach each child with special attention and assess the problem for appropriate diagnosis and treatment.

    ROP Screening

    ROP Screening

    Bardhaman Eye Care provides facilities for Retinopathy of Prematurity Screening, a blinding disease of the premature newborns. We screen babies who are born prematurely before 30 weeks or have low birth weight less than 1.5 kg or have other possible risk factors.

    Comprehensive Eye Care
    Comprehensive Eye Care

    Vision is one of the God's most beautiful gift. Routine eye check up is very important for early diagnosis of eye diseases and appropriate treatment.
    At Bardhaman Eye Care, a comprehensive eye examination is available to get a total evaluation of your eye health.
    So, all patients of above age 40, and family history of eye disease or history of systemic diseases or on long term medications should undergo comprehensive eye check up.
